Welcome to Casa Evaliza

Casa Evaliza is a spacious Bali rental villa, located 250 metres from the beach in the up market Seminyak area on Bali's southwest coast and accessed through the same road which serves the Club at The Legian Hotel, and just minutes walk from the world famous Ku De Ta restaurant.

Kitchen

The fully equipped, modern kitchen is strategically positioned between the Living & Dining Pavilion and the staff service area.

Swimming Pool & Garden

The central feature of the garden is a 14-metre swimming pool, with its white Yogya stone surround; it features a dazzling water cascade at one end.

Private Gym

Casa Evaliza offers an air-conditioned gym and in-villa spa services by trained therapists (extra charges apply).

Spa Services
